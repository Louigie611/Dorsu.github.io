QUALITY POLICY

DAVAO ORIENTAL STATE COLLEGE OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY is an educational institution established and sustained by quality education. It seeks to successfully implement and improve its Quality Management System (QMS) in order to better serve its students and the community, further contributing to nation building.

To sustain this culture of quality, DOSCST is committed to:

  • Deliver only quality education, research and extension to its stakeholders; Offer relevant and value-adding programs that are fit to the industry needs of the country;
  • Sustain a conductive environment for learning, research and extension through continuous training of its workforce and by making available sufficient facilities and other infrastructure;
  • Comply with all applicable legal requirements and other regulations it is obliged to adhere to; Serve its stakeholders better by implementing value-adding improvements using different tools such as Risk Management and 5S; and
  • Transfer organizational knowledge to other institutions that will benefit from DOSCSTâ€™s expertise in implementing a QMS.
